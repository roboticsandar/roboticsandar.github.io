Robots you must know if you are in the field of robotics

Robots that took the robotics field in whole new dimension and still their working ,algorithms and technology are in use.

Shakey (Intelligence Robot)

Shakey was the first mobile intelligence robot. It was developed at the Artificial Intelligence Center of Stanford Research Institute (now SRI International) and funded by DARPA.

Shakey represented true breakthroughs in natural language ability and computer vision. It’s A* search algorithm is still used today to plot paths between two points.

Unimate (Industrial Robot)

Unimate was the first industrial robot, the first robotic arm, and the first robot that did work once done by humans. Its inventor is George Devol, the technology was so new, Devol’s 1954 patent did not contain a single prior citation.

Unimate made its television debut in 1966, when it appeared on Johnny Carson and put a golf ball into a cup, poured a beer, and even conducted the Tonight Showband.

Sojourner (Rover)

Mars Pathfinder’s Sojourner rover was humanity’s first robotic mission to another celestial body. It landed on Mars on an auspicious day, July 4, 1997.

The semi-autonomous wheeled robot accepted general commands from NASA but could plan its own routes and avoid obstacles without prior detailed knowledge of its environment.
